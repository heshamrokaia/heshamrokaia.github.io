Key Insights

Regional Performance

The Western region continues to outperform other regions with 32% of total sales. However, the Eastern region is showing the highest growth rate at 28% year-over-year.

Product Category Analysis

Technology products are the top performers with 45% of revenue, followed by Office Supplies at 30% and Furniture at 25%. Technology also has the highest profit margin at 28%.

Customer Segment Trends

Corporate customers represent our largest segment at 38% of total sales. However, the Small Business segment is growing fastest at 34% year-over-year, indicating a market opportunity.

Recommendations

1. Increase marketing efforts in the Eastern region to capitalize on the strong growth trend.

2. Develop targeted promotions for technology products to the Small Business segment.

3. Implement a loyalty program for Corporate customers to maintain market share.
