Case Study: Call Center Process Improvement

Challenge

The call center was experiencing a high volume of repeat calls, with customers calling multiple times for the same issue. This was increasing operational costs and affecting customer satisfaction scores.

Solution

Led a team of seven to analyze over 200 repeat calls using a custom Excel tracking system with:

  • Advanced filtering to categorize call types and reasons
  • Pivot tables to identify patterns in repeat calls
  • Custom dashboard to visualize call volumes by reason, time of day, and agent
  • VBA macros to automatically generate daily reports for team leaders

Results

The analysis identified key drivers of repeat calls, including knowledge gaps, process breakdowns, and system limitations. Implementing the recommended changes resulted in:

  • 20% reduction in repeat calls within six months
  • 15% improvement in first-call resolution rates
  • Estimated annual savings of $120,000 in operational costs
